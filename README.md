This setup helps you interact naturally and get instant answers or assistance.

## ❓ Troubleshooting

- **App does not open:**
  - Check that your Windows is updated.
  - Try running as administrator (right-click > Run as administrator).

- **Microphone not working:**
  - Make sure your microphone is plugged in.
  - Check privacy settings in Windows: Settings > Privacy > Microphone > Allow apps to access.

- **Webcam issues:**
  - Confirm your webcam is connected and not used by another app.
  - Ensure Windows privacy settings allow camera use.

- **The app seems slow:**
  - Close other heavy programs.
  - Restart your PC and try again.
